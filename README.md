# Sprout Universe

Sprout Universe is a calm family of small daily-life tools made to help people
care for their routines with less pressure and more warmth.

The first live product is **Sprout Planner**, a gentle habit and task companion
where daily progress is represented through a small sprout world. The product is
designed for short, personal check-ins: planning today, finishing tasks,
reviewing progress, and keeping a soft sense of momentum.

## Purpose

Sprout Universe exists to make everyday self-management feel lighter. Instead
of turning productivity into pressure, rankings, or noisy gamification, it uses
clear routines, calm visuals, and encouraging feedback to help users return to
their day with a little more steadiness.

The product is built around a simple idea: progress should feel tended, not
forced.

## What Sprout Planner Does

Sprout Planner helps users:

- Plan a small set of daily tasks.
- Track completion without turning the day into a score chase.
- Build and review gentle streaks.
- See progress through a simple calendar view.
- Keep reminders and recurring routines close at hand.
- Use a soft visual companion as encouragement rather than pressure.

The experience is intentionally personal, lightweight, and calm.

## Design Direction

Sprout uses a warm, garden-like visual language: soft greens, friendly mascot
art, clear controls, and quiet motion. The interface is meant to feel tactile
and easy to return to, especially on a phone.

The product avoids heavy dashboard complexity, aggressive gamification, and
visual clutter. Its goal is to make daily check-ins quick, readable, and
emotionally gentle.

## Privacy Approach

Sprout Planner is designed around local, personal use. The app focuses on the
user's own routines, tasks, settings, and progress.

When optional assistant features are used, the relevant prompt content may be
sent to the configured assistant service so it can respond. Users should avoid
entering sensitive personal information into optional assistant features.
